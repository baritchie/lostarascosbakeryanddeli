A Proud Member of the Santa Barbara Community

Passion

Los Tarascos Bakery and Deli is a labor of love for all who work here. We believe in our foods, breads, and beverages, and we hope our commitment to our customers shows.

Fresh

We believe in using fresh ingredients that are locally sourced, and that's why our drinks and foods will always be freshly made with fresh ingredients from the best sources.

Giving Back

Through support of local arts, schools, and events, Los Tarascos believes in making a difference in the Santa Barbara community. We love it here just as much as you!
